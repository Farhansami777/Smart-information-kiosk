LABS INFRASTRUCTURE

Department of Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering Labs Infrastructure

The Department of Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering is spread over an area of 2706 square meters with well equipped six laboratories and six class rooms among which one is multimedia classroom (EI-417) and tutorial rooms for UG. The class rooms are having the facilities of Smart TV in First Year Classroom (EI-321) and LCD projectors for presenting the lectures and having sufficient space to can accommodate more than 60 students.

The department is having a sophisticated Library with a good collection of more than 1,806 Text & reference books.Department is also fully equipped with different cutting edge laboratories to give a proper hands on experience to the students.

Department library

The department is having a sophisticated Library with a good collection of more than 1,806 Text & reference books, IEEE Journals, Magazines, Periodicals, CD's, Project Reports, Laboratory Manuals, etc.

VLSI LAB

VLSI Lab is set up for Autonomous students with Cadence simulating tool and with Xilinx FPGA Trainer Kits with interface module, Modelsim Software, built with an area of 1031 sq.ft.

R&D LAB

The lab is equipped with latest simulators for ECG,EEG, EOG, Phlethysmography, Galvanic resistance system, Pacemaker system, Electrical Safety analyser, Audiometer. In all the equipments are provided with simulators and real time data acquisition is possible. It is built with an area of 107 sq. ft
Room num: EI-411

VI and DSA Lab

This lab is mainly built to practice NI Data acquisition Cards, NI Elvis, myRIO, myDAQ. Desktops & DSOs 100 MHz setup exists, with built up area of 891.sq.ft
Room num: EI-426

IAT Lab

This lab is mainly built to practice PLC, Level Control Trainer, Flow Control Trainer, Temperature Control Trainer, PID Controller Trainer, DC Postion Servo Mech Demo Unit, Inductive Transducer, Pressure Transducer Power Electronics Trainer kits, LCR Q Meters, M.,DSOs built with an area of 1138.sq.ft.
Room num: EI-423

Analog Electronics Lab

This lab mainly serves for CRO, DSO, Power Supply, Signal generator, Instrumentation Bridges, Instrumentation Trainer kits, LCR Q Bridge, Inductive transducer, Photo diode setup, Photo transistor setup, Capacitive pickup setup, Diaphragm type pressure transducer built with an area 1123.4 sq.ft.
Room num: EI-411
